My birthmark is a jagged line that stretches across my left shoulder, a bold streak of color that has been there since birth. Growing up, I called it my “warrior mark,” as it felt like a badge of honor. In high school, when I faced challenges—friend drama or tough exams—I would touch it for comfort. It became my reminder of resilience, a unique part of my story.
