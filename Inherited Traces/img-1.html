I have a heart-shaped mole on my right arm that has been with me for as long as I can remember. As I grew, the mole transformed, becoming darker and more pronounced, almost like a little tattoo. I was around 12 when I first noticed its heart shape, and it struck me as something special. It felt like a secret that only I knew, a tiny emblem of love nestled on my skin.
